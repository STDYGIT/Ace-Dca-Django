Privacy Policy

// Protecting your data with academic excellence

Your privacy is fundamental to our mission of providing quality academic resources.

Last updated: June 03, 2025

At Ace DCA, accessible from https://acedca.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by Ace DCA and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

When you sign in, we collect your basic public data to provide you with enhanced services. We do not collect any other form of data beyond what is necessary for platform functionality.

Usage Data

We may collect information on how the service is accessed and used, including but not limited to IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage patterns to improve our academic platform.

2. Use of Data

Ace DCA uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • • To provide and maintain our academic resource service
  • • To notify you about changes to our service and new resources
  • • To provide customer support and academic assistance
  • • To monitor the usage of our service and improve user experience
  • • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your academic data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics as a third-party service to monitor and analyze the use of our academic platform. Your interaction with Google Analytics is governed by their own terms and conditions. We may also integrate with educational platforms and services to enhance your learning experience.

5. Links to Other Sites

Our service may contain links to other educational sites, resources, or academic platforms that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: acedca.ac@gmail.com
